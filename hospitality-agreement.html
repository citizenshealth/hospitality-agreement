Hospitality Agreement

Citizens Associates

This Agreement is between Citizens Medical Center, Inc. (CMCI) ("Employer") and the undersigned Associate, for providing sleeping quarters during inclement weather or staffing needs of the organization.

Associate: ("Associate")

The sleeping room will be located at the North Campus. The room will be furnished with a bed and restroom. Access to the building will be added to the Associate's badge and is to be utilized by the Associate only. The rooms will be single occupancy.

Terms of Use

  • To request the use of a room to stay overnight the associate shall
  • The room will be available for short-term stays, no more than 3 nights typically. Specific arrangements may be made based on circumstances.
  • No hanging of any pictures or devices on the walls.
  • Notify Plant Operations immediately of mechanical conditions needing correction.
  • Linens shall be picked up from EVS. Upon leaving the room, the associate shall put all linens in a blue linen bag and take them to the Soiled Utility room.
  • Leave the premises clean and in the same condition as when first occupied. The associate shall take out all trash from the room and place it in the Soiled Utility room.
  • No alcohol use permitted on premises.
  • Absolutely no pets of any size, shape, or kind will be permitted in the rooms.
  • No tobacco use or any illicit substance shall be allowed in the rooms.
  • No electrical appliances will be permitted, to include: crockpots, space heaters, electrical blankets/mattress pads. Other electrical items such as curling irons and blow dryers will need to be assessed by Plant Operations on-call no later than 6 p.m. on the day of stay.
  • Citizens Health shall not be responsible for any lost or stolen items left unattended in the rooms.

Emergencies

  • All Associates staying in North Campus shall leave their cell phone number with the Nurses Station to be able to be reached in the event of any safety concerns.
  • In the event of a fire, the Associate shall evacuate the building and report to the South Campus Nurses Station.
